Toad in the hole

This is a new recipe we'll be trying out

Ingredients

  • Sausages - 8
  • Vegetable Oil - 3tbsp
  • Plain Flour - 100g
  • Eggs - 2 medium
  • Horseradish - 1tbsp grated
  • Milk - 225ml
  • Cherry Tomatoes - 200g

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celcius. Fry sausages in a non-stick pan until browned.
  2. Drizzle vegetable oil in a 30cm x 25cm x 6cm deep roasting tray and heat in the oven for 5 min.
  3. Put the sausages into the hot roasting tray and pour over the batter. Top with cherry tomatoes on the vine and cook for 30 minutes until puffed and golden.
